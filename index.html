The Computer Science Forum at AHS's STEAM Center is dedicated to the exploration of the distinct aspects of technology and programming.

Subforums

The CSF has a vast collection of distinct forums, each focused on their own field of study in computer science. The forum typically meets as a collective every Wednesday at 4:30 PM, but subforum meetings aren't always weekly. It's best to stay on track with your subforums in order to understand their schedules.
